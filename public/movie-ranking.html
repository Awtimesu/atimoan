My top three movies of all time

White Chick

I love this movie for its humor and unexpected plot twist. The characters are really loveable and amazing.

Detective Conan Movie

The movie is filled with suspense and thrilling action that keeps you wanting more and is overall a nice rendition from the manga.

Silent Voice

It is a japanese animated movie about a deaf girl and a main lead who is a bully that ended up getting depression. It follows the main lead and his journey for forgiveness.
